Jaipur,also known as the 'pink city' is a metropolitan city in the state of Rajansthan, India. There are magnificient  royal Rajputana monuments throughout the old Jaipur and Amer region but the decendents of erstwhile subjects have engulfed these royal places and have enriched their value by still showing their undying devotion and love towards their past rulers.
The 29th Kachwaha ruler Sawai Jai Singh II build the present day pink city in 1727. It was announced as a UNESCO World Heritage City in 2019.
The city has many popular tourist attractions but if planned in advance all the major places can be covered in 2 days.
