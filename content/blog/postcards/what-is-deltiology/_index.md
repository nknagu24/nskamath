## What is Deltiology?

Deltiology is the study and collection of postcards. It is one of the most popular collecting hobbies in the world, alongside philately (stamp collecting) and numismatics (coin collecting). The word comes from the Greek word *deltion*, meaning small writing tablet.

## A Brief History

Postcards first appeared in the late 19th century. The earliest known postcard dates to 1840, but it was the 1870s that saw their mass production and popularity explode across Europe and America. The period between 1900 and 1915 is often called the **Golden Age of Postcards**, when billions of cards were printed and exchanged worldwide.

## Why Collect Postcards?

- They are small, affordable and easy to store
- Each card is a tiny window into history, art and geography
- Handwritten messages on old cards offer intimate glimpses into everyday life
- They connect people across countries and cultures
